What makes Chance a hero?

Chance the Rapper changed the way the industry consumes art. His first three commercial releases were all independently released mixtapes, and for the first time in the streaming era, Chance showed that anyone could pursue their dream of music with success without the constraints of a record label.

Chance inspired a generatiion artists, including me. He was the artist that fueled my passion for music, amd allowed me to believe that I have all the tools to achieve my dreams.
